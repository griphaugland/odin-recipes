Chicken breast

Make the perfect chicken breast

Ingredients

  • 300g Chicken Breast
  • 1/2 Chili
  • 50g Ginger
  • 1 ts salt
  • 1 ts pepper

Steps

  1. Make rub with spices
  2. Put chicken in rub and leave in fridge
  3. Throw chicken on the grill
  4. Grill for 5 minutes on each side on 230 degrees
  5. Enjoy!
