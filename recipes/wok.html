Wok

The best Wok you can make

Ingredients

  • 400g noodles
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 chili
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • Cashew nuts
  • Koriander
  • Oyster sauce

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put noodles in water for 6 minutes
  3. Cut vegetables
  4. Strain water from noodles
  5. Add vegetables and oyster sauce to noodle pot
  6. Enjoy!
